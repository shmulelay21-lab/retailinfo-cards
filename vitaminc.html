"When do I use it — and can it mix with my other products?"
What is this?
A concentrated serum with Vitamin C that brightens skin, fades dark spots, and boosts collagen production.
What is it used for?
Applied in the morning after cleansing, before moisturizer. Evens skin tone, adds natural glow, protects from environmental damage.
Before buying, check:
Effective concentration is 10–20%. Store in a dark, cool place. If the serum turned orange or brown — it has oxidized, do not use. Check expiry date.
⚠ Warning
Do not mix with Retinol or Niacinamide in the same step. May cause mild tingling on first use. Always follow with SPF during the day.
Suitable for:
All skin types. Especially effective for dull or uneven skin. Sensitive skin — start with 10% concentration.
